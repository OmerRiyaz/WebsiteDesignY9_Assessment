Matterhorn
The Matterhorn is one of the most renowned mountain peaks in the world, and lies at the foot of the Swiss Alps. There are a number of routes to the summit, so this is a popular destination for mountaineers and hikers. The Matterhorn is also one of the world's most photographed peaks and it can be seen from several cities or villages in this area.
Zermatt
Zermatt, situated in the Swiss Alps, is one of the most famous ski resorts in the world. It is perhaps best known for its stunning view of the Matterhorn, one of the highest mountains in the world. During the winter season, Zermatt's ski and snowboard courses offer a wide variety of slopes suitable to all skill levels. There is a charming atmosphere with traditional Swiss chalets, modern hotels and luxury restaurants in the car free village of Zermatt. It's a gateway into the beautiful Gornergrat Railway, which gives an excellent view of the surrounding mountains and glaciers.
Rhine Falls
The Rhine Falls are the highest waterfall in Europe, located on the northern part of Switzerland and is close to the German border. Falls are a favorite tourist destination, and visitors can watch them from different vantage points, which include visits in boats that go directly to the falls. Several hiking paths, picnic areas and restaurants are also located in the surrounding area.
