Balloon Festival
Every year, the International Balloon Festival is held in January. Château-d’Oex hosts this festival as it is the capital of Swiss hot air balloons. This festival is attended by pilots from various countries and on these nine days of festivities hundreds of hot air balloons fly over the sky. If you plan a visit to Switzerland in January, it is recommended that the International Hot Air Balloon Festival should be on your travel schedule due to its reputation as one of the best festivals in Switzerland. If you are planning to visit Switzerland any time soon, then this festival will surely make you gather a few amazing memories.
Geneva Motor Show
The Geneva Motor Show is one of the world's best cat walks for cars, an annual event that takes place in mid March each year and constitutes a sort of ramp to new vehicles. For the first time in its history, a number of highly famous companies are showing their vehicles at this show to an international audience. You should buy a ticket for the Geneva Motor Show if you're going to watch these festivals in Geneva, Switzerland. This unique festival in Switzerland should be explored during your vacation, whether or not you care about motors.
Montreux Jazz Festival
New Orleans, the city where jazz was born and which it's become famous around the world today, has given birth to this intensified feeling of nonchalance. You must go to the largest jazz festival in Switzerland if you've been blown away by Jazz. The Montreux Jazz Festival, which takes place in July each year, is one of the world's biggest jazz festivals. This festival is attended by a lot of famous jazz performers, as well as emerging artists. Montreux Jazz Festival is an event not to be missed, and it will remain the most memorable moment in your life.Whether you have love for jazz or not, this energetic festival will surely make you dance to the tune with its amazing vibe!
