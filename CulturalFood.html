Cheesefondue
Cheese fondue is one of the most famous Swiss dishes and is a popular winter comfort food. The cheese used in fondue is typically a mixture of Gruyere and Emmental, although other types of cheese can also be used. The cheese is melted in a caquelon over a small flame and then served with cubed bread, potatoes and pickles for dipping. Fondue is often enjoyed with a glass of white wine.
Rosti
Rosti is a crispy potato dish that is typically served as a side dish. It consists of grated potatoes that are formed into a pancake shape and then fried until crispy on the outside and soft on the inside. Rosti can be served plain or with additional ingredients like bacon, cheese or onions.
Zurcher Geschnetzeltes
Zurcher Geschnetzeltes is a popular Swiss dish made with thin strips of veal that are cooked in a creamy mushroom sauce. The dish is usually served with rosti or pasta and is a hearty and comforting meal. It is typically enjoyed with a glass of Swiss red wine.
